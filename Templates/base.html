Welcome to the Empire Campaign Calculator
Welcome

This calculator is a tool to make your forays into the world of the Empire LARP Military Council downtime campaigns easier.

How to Use

Enter the forces and fortifications for both the Imperial and Barbarian sides of the campaign. The calculator will then determine the outcome of the campaign.

It handles all the various orders (with the exception of Let None Pass and No Matter the Cost) as well as rituals. Bound by Common Cause also has no direct impact whilst I implement Military Unit integration.
